Welcome to our dance society
...Eat dance sleep repeat...

I believe that at its core, dance stems from the joy of moving the body.

Hi there! I’m Nicole. My friends call me Nic.

I am a dancer, creator, choreographer, and visionary. I utilize movement as a form of expression to give life to my racing thoughts, ideas, and visualizations around me. I enjoy creating a world of shapes, line, and form — to destruct and re-build. My technique develops from an individual’s core experience and identity, exploring movement through the process of folding and unfolding.
